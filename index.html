Hi, I'm Marc and I like making games

Check out my CV

I'm a Multimedia Engineering student (4th year) who loves games and all game-related things. Here you can find all my repos and here you can find my LinkedIn profile. I mainly work in the areas of game design and programming, but I can make semi-decent art if the need arises.

Below you'll find some of my projects, I hope you find them interesting.

Grand Snail Prix

A local 1v1 racing game without speed. Winner of the GMTK Game Jam 2018.

Firedousers

A procedurally generated Rogue-Like where you control a firefighter who must navigate fire and hazards in order to save people. Still in development.

Star Creck

A tech demo using unity, made to test my art skills.

WebGL Experiment

A Mini-Game made with WebGL and Three.js where you control a car through falling platforms.

LS Dungeon Crawler

A web based dungeon crawler where you must navigate a maze and fight against college staff.

Styx Rescue

An endless runner where you must save people from going to hell by possessing them. This was an entry for the Not Only Games Jam by King 2018.

Troner

An online Java-based implementation of the motorbike game featured on the film Tron. It has an online lobby, rankings, 2v2, 4v4 and tournament modes.

Sky Racing

A tech demo of my very own C++ game engine. All features and visuals where implemented by me.
